How It Works

From Job Post to Hired — in 4 Simple Steps

No complex setup. No training needed. Just upload and let AI do the heavy lifting.

1

Register & Login

2

Upload Resumes

3

AI Screens & Scores

4

Auto-Invite Top Picks

Candidates scoring ≥75 are flagged for interview. AI writes the invite email for you.
